About us

 

EXODUS Tattoo and Piercings is an established tattoo and piercing parlour which is home to a number of talented and stand out artists whilst previously operating under the name Extreme tattoo and piercing for almost 12 years, EXODUS tattoo and piercings has proved that it's used to holding itself to a professional and quality standard, the only thing is new is the name and brand.

 

We are a well established tattoo studio in Inverness, Highlands, Scotland. In our studio we look to cater for everybody’s Tattoo needs with Artists specialising in Realistic, Japanese, Neotraditional, Traditional, Dotwork and Illustrative Tattooing. We bring many incredible Guest Artists to work in our studio each month to allow our Clients the opportunity to get Tattooed by the very best Artists specialising in their own styles and collect amazing pieces of work during their visits.

 

We have a dedicated Piercing set up in our studios with highly trained and experienced Piercer on hand to carry out Piercing of the highest quality .

What we do

  • Tattooing

    The tattoos of our artists will look beautiful not only right after the session, but also years later.

  • Tattoo cover up

    Do you have some old tattoos that you don't find pretty? Our talented artist will cover them for you.

  • Piercing

    Our master has experience in all areas of piercing (including exotic and dermal).
